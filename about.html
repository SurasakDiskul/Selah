ABOUT SELAH INTERNATIONAL

SELAH
INTERNATIONAL CO.,LTD.

We believe that good health begins with the choices we make every day. Our journey began with the intention of bringing high-quality products from Korea to Thailand, allowing people to access better choices for their health and well-being.

We coordinate the import of premium standard products from Korea, carefully selecting items that seamlessly integrate into daily beverage consumption and healthy lifestyles.

Through comprehensive support throughout the entire process, we help Thai entrepreneurs gain access to certified manufacturers, including factory visits for both product and machinery production facilities to ensure quality and manufacturing standards.

Our involvement extends from market research and product design to coordinating the import of high-quality products backed by research and innovation, delivered directly to entrepreneurs in Thailand.

Acting as a trusted intermediary and coordinator, we ensure that every step is carried out smoothly, efficiently, and with close attention to every detail.

Through our careful selection process, every product reflects our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation.

We are more than just an importer, we strive to be part of creating a healthier lifestyle for people in Thailand, not only through the products we introduce, but also through the trust, support, and dedication behind every step of the process.

SPECIAL EXCLUSIVE PARTNERS

Our company not only provides import coordination services for health-focused food and beverage products but also specializes in the sourcing and import coordination of industrial machinery solutions.

We collaborate with exclusive global partners in custom-made machinery manufacturing, including Samsung Mechatech from South Korea and VMS – Maschinenbau GmbH from Germany, to deliver fully integrated machinery solutions tailored to each client's specific production needs.

Through our partnership with Samsung Mechatech, a specialist in industrial automation systems and machinery from South Korea, we are able to offer advanced manufacturing technologies designed to improve production efficiency and operational performance.

Their expertise covers a wide range of industrial equipment, including Filling Machines, Packing Machines, and Robot Palletizers, allowing machinery to be precisely designed and customized according to customer requirements.
